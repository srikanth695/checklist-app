Python/Flask Logic Improvements
To support a modern UI, you should make the backend more "reactive." Here are a few logic-side suggestions:

Context Processors for Icons:
Instead of hardcoding emojis, use a helper to inject Material Symbols.

@app.context_processor
def inject_icons():
    return dict(icon_habit="cached", icon_journal="edit_note", icon_schedule="event")

Flash Message Categorization:
Material Design uses "Snackbars" for feedback. Update your Flask flashes to include categories that map to Material colors:

flash('Habit saved!', 'success') # Maps to green snackbar
flash('Error saving entry', 'error') # Maps to red snackbar
